Q.When is Antaragni'14?
The festival is from 9th - 12th October 2014(Thu- Sun).

Q.How can I participate in Antaragni'14?
You have to register for the festival through a two-stage process: Pre-Registration Final Registration(After reaching the I.I.T. Campus) For detailed procedure of Online Registration check out registration process

Q. We are a group of friends not participating in any of the major competitions and just coming to Antaragni to have fun. Would we be allowed?
No, we do not allow non-participating students.

Q.How many events can one participate in?
A participant once registered and his registration confirmed by the hospitality coordinators, can participate in any number of events. This excludes Ritambhara, MUN, Synchronicity and Junoon. For these, one can contact the respective event coordinators.

Q.What to do in case you are not able to register online?
A. Try opening the registration portal in Internet explorer (6.0 or higher). If it does not fix the problem talk to repective coordinators.

About registration process

Q.Who is a team leader?
A Team Leader will be the sole point of contact between the Hospitality Team and the respective college for all the formalities. He or She will be responsible for the actions of the participants from his/her college. For more details on the selection of a Team Leader, please go through the pre-registration process.

Q. What is an N.O.C.?
An N.O.C is a No Objection Certificate; a document from the official authorities of the participating colleges (printed on an official Letterhead of the college and bearing the Authorized Signatures), stating the details of the approved Team from the participating college. These details shall be crosschecked with the Identity Proofs produced at the time of Final Registration.

Q.I am unable to get an N.O.C.(No objection Certificate) from my college,can I still Participate?
NO, if a team/individual participant fails to provide an N.O.C. at the time of Final Registration, he/she shall not be registered for the festival.

Q. My online registration status is still "pending", when will it be confirmed?
Contact the team leader of your college and ask him/her to approve your name on the list. After the team leader's approval the hospitality coordinators will confirm your registration and a confirmation mail will be sent to you.

Q.When will my accommodation be confirmed?
Once your online registration is confirmed and the team size is ratified by the hospitality coordinators then your accommodation will be confirmed .And the confirmation will be communicated to the Team Leader. In case of any disputes, the decision of the Hospitality Coordinators would be final and binding.

Q.What kind of accommodation will be provided?
Accommodation will be provided to all the registered participants on the same lines as the one provided to the students staying on the campus.

Q.How do I reach I.I.T. Kanpur?
We have a bus service from the railway station but due to the limited availability of buses, a team may have to take an auto-rickshaw which charges around Rs.150.

Q. Are separate arrangements made for accommodation of professors?
Separate accommodation arrangements for faculty are subjected to availability and hence it cannot be guaranteed.

Q.I do not wish to take the accommodation or I am staying only for a day, can my registration fee be relaxed?
No, it is mandatory for the participants to take the accommodation as we want the students to be a part of the Festival over all four days.

Q. Till when can I keep the accommodation allotted to me?
You are supposed to move out of the rooms by 08:00 hrs. On 13th October.

Antaragni is the Annual Cultural Festival of IIT Kanpur. The festival was originally known as 'Culfest' which gave the generic name to college festivals in India. It was later re-christened to Antaragni in 1993.

Antaragni literally meaning the 'Fire Within' is a celebration of the creative spirit and youthful energy inherent in the country's college students.
